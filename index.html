Battleship Instructions

Welcome to Battleship! The goal of the game is to sink all of the ships by guessing the location on a grid.

Gameplay

The computer will start with a fleet of three ships placed on a 9x7 grid. You will then place bombs by clicking on cells.

If a ship is located at the guessed coordinate, it is considered a "hit". This will be represented by pink bomb icon. If the guess is a miss, this will br represented by a blue X. Once a ship sinks, the icons will change to a dark red ship symbol.

The number of tries it takes you to sink the ships is recorded. Try and keep it as low as possible!

Getting Started

Click one of the "Begin" buttons below to select ship size and start playing Battleship.
